About Us

Welcome to the official website of Synapse At UCI! We are a group of enthusiastic students who share a passion for neuroscience and cognitive science. Our club offers various opportunities for students to explore the fascinating world of brain research and cognitive psychology.

Follow us on Instagram: UCI Synapse

Activities

Throughout the school year, we organize a variety of activities and events, including:

  • Guest lectures from renowned neuroscientists
  • Hands-on brain dissection workshops
  • Neuroscience-themed movie nights
